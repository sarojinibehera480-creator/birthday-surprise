Thank You, PUJA NANI (MAINSHI)

Thank you for treating me as your own brother.

The day you get married, I know I’ll smile for you… but a part of my heart will quietly miss having you so close.

Watching you start a new life will make me proud, even if my eyes get a little wet that day.

You may belong to a new home then, but you will always remain my sister, forever. No matter whose house you live in, my care, concern, and love for you will never change.

That day I won’t cry because I’m losing you, but because I’m grateful I was lucky enough to have you as my sister. Wherever life takes you, remember there will always be a brother here whose heart is proud of you.
